    Project idea

    Overview

    The Idea I have is a productivity bot for Discord. With technology ever increasing, Discord has taken one of the top spots for online voice communication applications. Not only can you connect with voice, but you can also have text-based channels to enhance the experience with connecting people from around the world. With these text-based channels you can create bots that can perform actions you design it to perform. For example, many bots exist that can play a song from YouTube and the bot will join a voice channel and begin playing the selected song in the voice chat for others to listen. My idea is to create a bot that will help students and basically anyone to improve on their productivity online.

    Motivation

    With 250 million users on Discord, there would be so many people that can benefit from this idea. Personally, I struggle keeping motivated or managing my time during a study period, to me a bot like this would be a huge game changer for me as I have something to remind me of important assignments coming up for example. I’m also a user of Discord, I use this application to connect with my friends overseas or play games with my local friends with voice communication. I’ve been very interested in coding and creating applications/bots, so this seems like a perfect idea for me.

    Description

    I hope to implement enough features into this bot to maximize efficiency and increased productivity rates. One feature I plan to implement is a timer that will for example, countdown from 1 hour and tell you to take breaks in between. This is a very simple but effective feature, you can get carried away and forget to eat or drink or just lose track of time so this feature will keep things like this in check. Another feature I’m planning on adding is a reminder function, this feature will be able to take commands to set a reminder for a certain time on a certain day, or even recurring. For example, I could have an assignment due on 23rd March 18:00. The bot would take this information and when that time arrives it would display a message to the creator of the reminder a week, a day, an hour before the assignment is due. Again, very simple but effective. It would be able to track many different users reminders and timers and if there is many users on the same server I could make it instead of using public text channels, it would use direct messages to the bot for clutter management. Another feature I plan to add is the ability for the bot to fetch wiki links related to the command you give it. For example, I want to search on wiki about IT, I would send it a command such as: !bot wiki Information technology. This would reply with a link to a wiki page related to IT. You might be asking, it’s just as fast if you look this up yourself? This may be true but the entire idea around this project is, if you’re already a big user of Discord and you use it often, this bot would enhance your experience even further from being an all-in-one application to talk and connect to a study environment that assists you with said study. What if you don’t have a calculator at hand? You can simply request the bot to do a calculation. Give it an equation for example, “!bot calc 5 / 2 * 4 + 3” and It will respond with 13. I’m not planning creating a fancy bot that will make users jaw drop but keeping it simple, tidy and efficient. The idea is just to improve your studying experience.
